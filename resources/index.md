## Software and Code Repositories

## DNA Assembly Resources

- [**Chloroplast MoClo Collection**](https://github.com/ChlamyMarburg/ChloroplastMoClo)  
  A modular cloning toolkit for engineering *Chlamydomonas reinhardtii* chloroplast genomes. The collection includes standardized parts, assembly vectors, and documentation for construct design.

- [**Addgene Distribution Page**](https://www.addgene.org/kits/erb-chloromodas/)  
  The Chloroplast MoClo kit is available through Addgene for distribution to the research community.
